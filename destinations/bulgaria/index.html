The Eastern City of Sofia, Bulgaria

  • March 26, 2017 •
  • Bulgaria

After Bucharest and Belgrade, Sofia is the third-biggest city in the Balkans. Home to over one million people, the city felt massive, especially arriving from the smaller cities of Skopje in Macedonia and Tirana in Albania. The city blocks in the center seemed never-ending, with lots to see, so off we went.
